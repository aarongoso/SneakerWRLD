About SneakerWRLD

SneakerWRLD is not just another sneaker store; it's a passion project founded by a visionary college student in 2023 who saw the world of footwear through a unique lens. What began as a dorm room dream has transformed into a thriving online destination for sneaker enthusiasts and fashion-forward individuals.

Our Vision

What sets SneakerWRLD apart is our commitment to showcasing not only popular and iconic sneaker brands but also uncovering hidden gems, limited editions, and custom designs that you won't find in mainstream stores. We take pride in offering a platform that reflects the diversity and creativity of the sneaker culture.

Refund/Return policy

We offer a 30-day redress policy. We do not offer cash or card refunds instead we will offer a credit note (gift card) to the value of your order which can be used at any time to purchase another product(s) from our website. This does not effect your statutory rights.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the exact same condition that you received it in, unworn or unused with tags and in it's original packaging which also must be undamaged. You'll need a receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, you can contact us through email at SneakerWRLD@gmail.com or via direct message on Instagram @SneakerWRLD. Once you meet the criteria for a return we will send you instructions on how to return the order and details on where to send the package, The cost of the shipping for the return will be on the customer.
